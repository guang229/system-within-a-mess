        point where we have submitted assignments late, contemplated skipping class, and ruined our sleep schedules, 

        just so we could play a round of 'Spit'. Unfortunately, as sophomores in fashion and communication design majors, 

        we have not been given the same luxury of time that we had previously. However, thankfully for the both of us,

        we were able to satiate our craving by spending time playing card games when she visited my apartment over the 
        
        weekend. I have missed both Pema and our card games so dearly.
