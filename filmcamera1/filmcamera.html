        One of my many cameras, this is one that I've been using since 2021. I started doing film photography in 
        
        2020 as a habit to keep me occupied during COVID. Over the years film photography has become one of my 
        
        most constant, favourite, and expensive art mediums to work with. I've gone over well over 100 film rolls
        
        (yikes!) and counting (even more yikes!). I've resonated so much with film simply because it's such a "in
        
        the moment" medium. Having perfectionist tendencies, I often fixate too much on the small details, and as 
        
        I'm writing this, I have changed the spacing, colour, and alignment of this very text several times. But 
        
        fixation is fundamentally impossible when it comes to shooting film. Sure you can consider the composition
        
        and lighting, but at the end of the day anything could really happen with film. It can come out ten times
        
        worse or better than what you planned (if you even plan), and that's something I've come to love. Anyhoo,
